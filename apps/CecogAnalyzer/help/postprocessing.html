Plots and Post Processing

This section is a general container for various post processing steps special to certain assys such as IBB analysis.

IBB-Analysis

The ratio between the of the secondary inside and outside region meassured on the IBB marker is used to determine the Nuclear Envelope Break Down (NEBD) and the IBB-Onset. Together with cell cycle information from the primary channel (Chromatin) various timing meassures are extracted, such as the timing from NEBD to the Separation event. For more information see: Live-cell imaging RNAi screen identifies PP2A–B55a and importin-ß1 as key mitotic exit regulators in human cells
